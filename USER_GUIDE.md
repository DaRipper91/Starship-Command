# User Guide

Welcome to the Starship Theme Creator!

## How to use the app

1. **Modules**: Use the Left Sidebar to manage your active and inactive modules. Drag and drop them to reorder your prompt. Toggle the checkboxes to turn them on or off.
2. **Colors**: Extract a color palette from an image using the "Colors" section in the left sidebar.
3. **Module Configuration**: Select a module from the left to configure it in the Right Sidebar. You can change its symbol, style, format string, and specific settings.
4. **Command Palette**: Press `Cmd + K` (or `Ctrl + K`) to open the Command Palette to search for actions.
5. **Theme Gallery**: Click the "Gallery" button in the top header to browse presets or your saved themes.
6. **Compare**: Click "Compare" in the header to view your current theme alongside another side-by-side.

## Exporting

When you are ready, click "Export" to download your `starship.toml` file, or copy it to the clipboard. Place it in `~/.config/starship.toml` to use it in your terminal.
