## 💡 PRO-TIPS FOR GENERATION

1.  **"No Text" is Law:** Always include "no text" or "no words" in your negative prompts. AI is great at art but terrible at spelling "Starship."
2.  **Solid Backgrounds:** If you need a transparent icon, ask the AI for a "solid flat background." It makes it much easier to use a "Background Remover" tool later.
3.  **Adaptive Shapes:** Android icons work best when the central "glyph" (the rocket) is centered and has some "padding" around it. Don't let the art touch the very edges of the square.
4.  **Dark Mode First:** Since Starship is a terminal tool, your assets should always be "Dark Mode" friendly. Avoid bright white backgrounds for icons.
